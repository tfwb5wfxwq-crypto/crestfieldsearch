About

Crestfield Search was founded in 2011 by partners who had grown frustrated with the limitations of large, generalist search firms. We saw a need for a more focused approach—one rooted in deep sector expertise, long-term relationships, and an understanding of the nuances that define success in financial services leadership.

We work exclusively on retained mandates for senior appointments in private equity, investment banking, hedge funds, and credit. Our assignments span London, New York, Paris, Frankfurt, and other major financial centres. We do not handle volume recruitment. Every engagement is treated as a partnership, conducted with the discretion and rigour our clients expect.

What distinguishes us is not the size of our firm, but the quality of our network and the depth of our market insight. Our team has held senior roles in investment banking, private equity, and executive search at leading institutions. We understand the challenges our clients face because we have navigated them ourselves.
